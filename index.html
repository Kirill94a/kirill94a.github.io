I am a PhD student at Perm National Research Polytechnic University, working with professor Alexander Yuzhakov. I am broadly interested in Machine Learning, Computer Vision, AutoML, and Meta-Learning.

My current research solves the problem of searching the optimal neural network architecture for the image classification task. As part of this research, I am creating my own meta-learning algorithm that adapts the weights of a neural network for a new task with few-shot learning, and also uses the NAS to create its optimal architecture.
